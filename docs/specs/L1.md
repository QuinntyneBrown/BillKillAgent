# L1 Requirements — Bill Kill Agent UI Design

## L1-1: Onboarding Flow
The app shall provide a multi-step onboarding flow that guides users from sign-up through bank connection, transaction scanning, savings discovery ("aha moment"), and action approval — delivering immediate value within minutes.

## L1-2: Dashboard Screen
The app shall provide a main dashboard displaying total recurring spend, total savings found, savings achieved to date, and quick-access to pending actions — serving as the central hub of the experience.

## L1-3: Subscriptions List Screen
The app shall provide a subscriptions list showing all detected recurring charges with value scores, usage indicators, categories, costs, and recommended actions (cancel, negotiate, switch, keep).

## L1-4: Action Queue Screen
The app shall provide an action queue showing pending actions awaiting approval, in-progress actions, and completed actions — enabling one-tap approval and status tracking.

## L1-5: Negotiation Log Screen
The app shall provide a negotiation log showing the history of all AI negotiations with outcomes, provider details, original vs. new rates, and savings achieved per negotiation.

## L1-6: Savings Tracker Screen
The app shall provide a savings tracker showing cumulative savings over time with charts, savings destination info, and monthly/annual breakdowns.

## L1-7: Settings Screen
The app shall provide a settings screen for managing connected accounts, notification preferences, savings destination, autonomy level (supervised/semi-autonomous/fully autonomous), and account/profile settings.

## L1-8: Alerts & Notifications
The app shall display alert banners, push notification previews, and in-app notification center for price increases, trial expirations, renewal dates, negotiation outcomes, and monthly reports.

## L1-9: Responsive Layout
All screens shall be fully responsive from x-small (320px) through x-large (1440px+) breakpoints, adapting layout, navigation, and content density appropriately.

## L1-10: Navigation & Global Chrome
The app shall provide consistent global navigation (bottom tab bar on mobile, sidebar on desktop) with access to all major screens, user avatar, and notification badge.
